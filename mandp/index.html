Three sets of metrics are available for both, margins and paddings:

1px, 2px, 3px......9px

10px, 20px, 30px......90px

100px, 110px, 120px......300px

.m-6Means 6px margin in all directions
.m-r-6Means margin right 6px
.m-l-6Means margin left 6px

.m-t-X means top and .m-b-Xmeans bottom

It is same for paddings, just replace m with p, e.g. .p-t-10 will mean padding top 10px
